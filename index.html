Hi, I'm Stefan Wehrmeyer.

I'm a software engineer and data activist working in journalism.

Currently I work for the non-profit investigative center Correctiv.org.

I fight for Freedom of Information on the board of the Open Knowledge Foundation Germany.

I'm working on reproducible data investigations and database news apps, I'm interested in EU farm subsidies and public transport data and I'm involved in Hacks/Hackers Berlin.

You can find me on GitHub and Twitter.
